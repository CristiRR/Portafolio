Star Wars : "The rise of skywalker"

Synopsis

It is the final installment of the Skywalker saga and the third film in the sequel trilogy. The movie follows Rey, Finn, and Poe Dameron as they lead the remnants of the Resistance in a final stand against the First Order, now led by Supreme Leader Kylo Ren. The stakes are raised when the resurrected Emperor Palpatine returns, threatening the galaxy once more. Rey keeps searching her place in the galaxy and questions more about her family, specialy her mother and father. Kylo Ren will also star questioning about his destiny since he is staring to feel conflicted by his emotions towards Rey.

Main characters

  • Rey: The protagonist, a Jedi in training who constanly seeks to uderstand her past and place in the galaxy.
  • Kylo Ren/Ben Solo: Leader of the First Order, struggling with his dark and light sides.
  • Poe Dameron: A skilled pilot and leader in the Resistance.
  • Finn: A former stormtrooper turned Resistance fighter.
  • Leia Organa: The General of the Resistance and a Key figure in the fight against the First Order.
  • Emperor Palpatine: The resurrected Sith Lord who poses a new threat to the galaxy.
  • Chewbacca: A Wookie and co-pilot of the Millennium Falcon.
  • R2-D2: A droid
  • BB-8: Poe's droid
  • C3PO: A droid and R2's best friend
